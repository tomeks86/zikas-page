Prof. Katarzyna Marta STADNICKA

Scientific interests
  • Crystallography, Crystal Chemistry and Crystal Physics, X-ray Diffraction
  • Structural analysis of single crystals containing small organic molecules or inorganic compounds;
    ferroic crystals and minerals
  • Structure - property relationships: physical (e.g. optical activity, SHG), chemical (e.g. solvatochromism, sorption, fluorescence),
    biological (e.g. antiarrhythmic, adrenergetic or serotonic activity, biocompatibility)
