Research areas


Structural aspects of physical, chemical and biological properties:
  • crystal structure analysis
  • experimental charge density
  • crystal engineering
  • molecular packing
  • intermolecular interactions
  • structure-property relationship for linear (OA, birefringence) and nonlinear (SHG) optical properties
  • structural mechanisms of phase transitions in ferroics
  • structure-pharmacological activity relationship for compounds showing antiarrhythmic and/or adrenergetic properties
  • strucure of minerals
